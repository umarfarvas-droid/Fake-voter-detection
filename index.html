About the Problem

Fake or duplicate voters undermine trust in democratic processes. Without reliable verification, elections can be skewed by fraudulent registrations, impersonation, and repeated voting under different identities.

  • Fraudulent or duplicate registrations inflate voter rolls.
  • Identity impersonation allows ineligible or repeated voting.
  • Manual checks are slow, error-prone, and hard to scale.
  • Weak audit trails make it difficult to detect abuse after the fact.

Features

Core capabilities designed for secure, verifiable voting.

Biometric Authentication

Bind each voter to strong biometric signals to reduce impersonation and stolen credentials.

AI Duplicate Detection

Machine learning flags likely duplicates across records, photos, and behavioral patterns.

QR Code Verification

Quick, tamper-aware checks at the polling station using signed QR payloads.

Secure Database

Encrypted storage, access controls, and logging for a defensible audit trail.

Prototype Flow

A simple mock of how a voter moves through the system.

  1. 1

    Scan voter

    Capture ID or QR and load the voter record at the booth.

  2. 2

    Verify identity

    Match biometrics or credentials against the authoritative register.

  3. 3

    Detect duplicate

    Run AI and database checks to ensure this vote has not already been cast.

  4. 4

    Allow / Reject vote

    Approve the ballot or block with a logged reason for auditors.
